Guelph Coding Community

Level up.

Learn

Our workshops are designed by passionate students and guest speakers to teach you a new skill. Come out and pickup a new tool to add to your arsenal. These workshops are targeted at students who share our love of computer science.

Code

We host hackathons to allow you to show off your particular skill set and compete with others. Apply the skills that you picked up at workshops to real world problems. Hackathons bring the community together and reward those who excel.

Teach

Have a passion that you want to share? Talk to us about running your very own workshop or helping to host a hackathon. We think that everyone is a teacher and are always looking for experienced students to expand our curriculum.
